Right mouse button - autoaim. Keep in mind that autoaim restriction is turned on by default and it means that you still need to aim roughly or turn restrictions off.

C - team pings. B - emotes. Because right mouse is used for autoaim.

Mouse wheel - zoom. Use 1, 2, 3, 4 to switch weapons.

Shift - display enemy names.
